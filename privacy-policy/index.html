Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Youkeo Studios ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website youkeostudios.com and use our services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our website.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly:

  • Contact Forms: When you submit a booking or contact inquiry, we collect your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Communications: If you contact us via WhatsApp, email, or phone, we collect the information you provide in those communications.

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. This includes your IP address, device type, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Google Analytics uses cookies to track your usage patterns.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your booking inquiries and provide recording studio services
  • To send you confirmations, updates, and customer support messages
  • To improve and optimize our website and services based on user behavior
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To prevent fraud and maintain website security

4. Advertising & Google AdSense

Our website uses Google AdSense to display personalized advertisements. Google AdSense may collect and use your information (including cookies) to show relevant ads based on your interests.

How Google Uses Your Information:

  • Google uses cookies and tracking pixels to collect browsing data
  • This data is used to serve ads relevant to your interests
  • You can opt out of personalized ads by visiting Google's Ads Settings

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Analyzes website traffic and user behavior
  • Google AdSense: Displays targeted advertisements
  • Google Fonts & CDN: Delivers website content and assets

These third parties may collect information about your visit independently. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies for more information.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information (name, email, phone) only as long as necessary to provide our services and respond to your inquiries. Typically, we retain contact form data for up to 2 years unless we need it for legal or service purposes.

Google Analytics data is retained according to Google's data retention settings, typically 14-26 months.

7. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: You can request to correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt out of personalized ads through Google's Ads Settings
  • Cookie Management: You can control cookies through your browser settings

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@youkeostudios.com or +91 9619192992.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information promptly.
