FAQ

Got questions?
We've got answers.

How long does a first session usually take? +

Most first-time clients book 2 hours — enough time to warm up, get comfortable in the booth, and walk out with a take you're genuinely happy with. If you're recording more than one song, 3–4 hours is safer. We'd rather you leave satisfied than rushed.

What do I need to bring? +

Just your voice and your lyrics. If you have a beat or reference track, send it to us the day before and we'll have it loaded and ready when you walk in. We handle the rest.

Can I bring my own tracks or beats? +

Absolutely. You can bring your beat in any common format — MP3, WAV, or a project file if it's a DAW we support. Send it to us in advance and we'll have it ready to go when you walk in.

What's your cancellation policy? +

We take a 20% advance to confirm your booking. Cancellations made 24+ hours in advance can be fully rescheduled at no extra charge. Same-day cancellations forfeit the advance. Studio-initiated cancellations receive a full refund.

How do I reach you from Thane, Vashi, Powai or Mulund? +

We're in Sector-19, Airoli — 8 minutes from Airoli Railway Station on the Harbour Line. From Thane it's ~20 minutes by road via Thane-Belapur Road. From Vashi, ~25 minutes. From Powai and Mulund, 15–25 minutes depending on traffic. Free parking right outside the studio.

Do you work with first-time artists or beginners? +

Yes — a big part of what we do. Our engineer guides you through warm-ups, helps you settle in the booth, and punches in takes so you get a clean performance even if it's your first time on a real mic. No judgment, no rushing.

Will my track sound like a professional release? +

That's the bar. Tracks recorded here have passed 1 million streams on Spotify and been placed on New Music Friday India and other editorial playlists. We use a Neumann U87, treated acoustics, and an engineer who's mixed and mastered for major releases — so the raw performance, the mix, and the final master translate on every speaker and platform.

Can you mix a song I've already recorded elsewhere? +

Yes. Send us your stems (or a stereo rough if stems aren't available) and we'll quote based on the scope. Stereo mixes start at ₹5,000 and stem mixes from ₹12,000, with 2 revision rounds and streaming-ready masters included.
